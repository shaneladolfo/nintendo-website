Logo Variations

These logos drew inspiration from themes from my personal mind map such as hard-working and friendly. I wanted to appear professional yet kind.

Color Logo

This full color logo is used for decorating any page or print in a less professional but more fun way. It can be used against a white background.

Black and White Logo

This is the main logo that I will be using for the website and other professional documents. It can be used for light green or white backgrounds.

Black and White Inverse Logo

This Black and White Inverse logo can be used to evoke professionalism and is an option to use for dark colored backgrounds.

Color Inverse Logo

The Color Inverse Logo is an option for less professional more relaxed pages and prints. This can be used against either a black or white background.

Favicon

This Favicon logo is used for the tab on a webpage. It will be used throughout the whole website.
