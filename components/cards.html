Cards

Cards are used within this design system to link to examples of work. Cards include an image, category, project title, and summary describing the content, as well as a link to the content.

Accessibility Considerations

Include meaningful alt text for card images. Make sure typography follows the typograhical hierarchy. Ensure links are stated with clear understanding for the user what they are clicking on.

Usage

Cards may be used to link to project content on the website.
